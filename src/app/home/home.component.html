Greco Law Associates P.C.

Greco Law Associates is a Northeastern Pennsylvania based law firm, which concentrates its practice in areas of the law including business and corporate law, employment law, estate administration and tax planning, civil litigation, state and federal taxation, real estate and municipal law. The current lawyer members of the firm represent over 60 years of experience. Carl J. Greco, the founder of Greco Law Associates P.C., has over 36 years of experience as a practicing lawyer in Northeastern Pennsylvania, having been admitted to the practice of law in Pennsylvania in 1978. The aforementioned areas in which the firm practices include matters relating to general contract law, financing, as well as drafting.
